ABOUT US //

Our family business originated in 1977 when the now deceased Mr. Javed Alam commenced the production and exportation of leather and non-leather saddlery, harnesses, and pet products.

In 1988, he went on to establish Eastern Saddlery. Nowadays, Faraz Alam, his son, leads the company in its latest avatar as Leather Art.

We now manufacture assorted Leather goods and non Leather articles. Though the core of our business still remains manufacturing Leather Saddlery goods.
